MESSAGE FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

The world is going through a tumultuous phase. Reminiscent of the early 20th century, international conflicts only seem to be spreading, each inextricably linked with the other. Political leaders across the world are dictators and tyrants all but in name, and humanitarian crises are taking lives indiscriminately across the globe. Ideological clashes, both economic and religious are cutting across countries and continents, leaving behind the ruins of devastated families and barren lands.


In an environment where conflict and capitalising on the conflicts seem to be the only truth. In a world that has been paralysed by a raging pandemic, diplomacy proves to be an effective mitigator in this global turmoil. CVMUN has always believed in collective growth and we strive to discuss agendas that will have a positive impact on our society. With this edition of the conference we wish to create an impact that will be remembered.


We, the organising committee, invite you to Respond, Resolve and revolutionise the world around you.


Nishant Kumar

Secretary-General

About CVMUN

Chettinad Vidyashram Model United Nations is an academic simulation of the United Nations where students play the role of delegates from different countries and attempt to solve real world issues with the policies and perspectives of their assigned country.
